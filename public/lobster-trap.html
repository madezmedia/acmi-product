Recording workflow (for the two video deliverables)

  • Sizzle: Open sizzle.html, hide playback bar via CSS or fullscreen, hit ▶, screen-record at 1080×1920. Auto-loops; one clean pass is the export.
  • Slideshow demo: Open slideshow-demo.html, fullscreen, hit ▶. The VO captions are burned-in at the bottom-third — Mikey reads them in time as you screen-record. 4:50 total, fits the ≤5min cap with buffer.
  • Cover image: Open cover.html, use the browser's "Capture screenshot" devtool (or any 1280×720 viewport capture) to export PNG.
  • Pitch deck PDF: Open pitch-deck.html, press Cmd/Ctrl+P → Save as PDF (landscape, 0 margins). Deck-stage handles one-page-per-slide automatically.
